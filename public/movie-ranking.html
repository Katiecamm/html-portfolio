Katie Camm's Top 3 Movies

The movies that have made me, shaped me, and changed me for the better

Moonrise Kingdom

A lesser appreciated Wes Anderson movie, Moonrise Kingdom captivated my heart and made me feel so seen at a time when I felt so alone. Throughout the years I have found myself time and time again in it's world, with my relationship to it's story and characters evolving as I got older.

Howl's Moving Castle

This movie came into my life when I needed it the most. It showed me how someone who truly loves you will treat you, how they will give to you as much as you give to them. I saw a whole other life unfold in front of me. I had hope for myself and my future again, something that had been dead for some time. It changed the course of my life.

The Iron Giant

An animated cinematic masterpiece. Instant classic.
